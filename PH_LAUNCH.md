## Description

IndieMetrics is the auto-updating public dashboard for indie makers who believe transparency builds trust.

Stop screenshotting Stripe every Monday. Connect your data sources once, then share a live dashboard that updates itself — while you sleep.

**The Open Startup movement is growing.** Founders who share metrics publicly grow 3x faster on X. IndieMetrics makes it effortless.

**Supported integrations:**
- 💳 Stripe (MRR, customers, churn)
- 🛒 Gumroad (sales, revenue)
- 📱 RevenueCat (mobile subscriptions)
- ⭐ GitHub (stars, forks)

**Key features:**
- 📊 Live public dashboard (embed anywhere)
- 📈 Revenue growth charts
- 🏆 Milestone timeline
- 🔗 Custom domain support
- 🎨 DSGVO-ready analytics
